We’re a grooving Hong Kong DJ agency from France.

About

Lullaby Tunes can provide a 360° solution for your musical needs. Whether you’re planning an event for your company, celebrating a special occasion or looking to give your venue a musical identity,
Call us music strategists, cultural connectors, or pulse finders, we leverage the power of music to make deep connections with consumers.

Our Services

Corporate events
Audio Branding
Weddings and personal parties
Professionalism first.
Product launch, corporate parties, grand openings or boat parties: our DJs deliver an elegant European flair with a highly professional attitude. From advising on the most suitable sound system to booking the proper DJ, we work with great companies to help them make their event a success.
Emotive Audio Content Builds Brand Identity
Lullaby Tunes works with top brands, stores and hospitality venues to create the perfect synergy for your place. On top of catering a precise sound, we understand that smart use of music and avoiding copyrights violation are always the issues for marketers. Be it in-store mixes, commercial scoring or developing a live set that speaks your message, music sets the tone for every experience.
A great party starts with great music.
We help turn your party into an unforgettable moment. Lullaby Tunes listen to your need to define what music will resonate the best with you and your closed ones. We strive to be professional, flexible and adaptive to make sure you get the atmosphere you need and the dance floor your party deserves.
